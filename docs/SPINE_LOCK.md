## üìù Core Narrative Structure

### Dual Narrative System
- **95% Closure / 5% Unease:** Cases solved but compass anomalies haunt the player
- **Dual Pursuit:** Explicit case hunts + implicit shadow hunts through compass anomalies
- **Season Structure:** 12 seasons, 144 episodes (5-minute micro-episodes, 2-3 scenes each)

### Character Ensemble (Futurama Uncharted Vibes)

#### Player (Gasket's Replacement) - "Compass Master"
- **Primary Role:** Inherits haunted Crimson Compass with pirate-tech aesthetic
- **Development Arc:** Grows from novice to master of ancient technology
- **Personality:** Brings professional sanity to dysfunctional brilliance
- **Compass Integration:** Primary compass user across all episodes, episode-specific specializations
- **Background:** Experiences "memory echoes" of Gasket's trauma

#### Optimus (Agent Y) - "The Bumbling Boomer Executive"
- **Core Trait:** Precision genius outpaced by technology
- **Personality:** Fumbles quantum communicators like rotary phones, calls AI "those talking computers"
- **Role:** Strategic brilliance despite technological struggles
- **Compass Role:** Sarcastic data processor attempting statistical analysis of anomalies

#### Helix (Agent X) - "Big Sister Administrative Glue"
- **Core Function:** Spunky coordinator who holds the team together
- **Personality:** Mediates arguments, manages logistics, maintains morale with warmth and administrative warfare
- **Role:** Creates "emotional support protocols" disguised as strategy meetings
- **Compass Role:** No-nonsense team coordinator ensuring safety during investigations

#### ZTech (Agent Z) - "Nerd Soldier Tech Genius"
- **Capabilities:** Commands 250 AI agents and 5000 robots in secret facility
- **Personality:** Over-engineered gadgets with absurd functionality (laser pointers that brew coffee)
- **Philosophy:** "Maximum absurdity, maximum function"
- **Compass Role:** Cocky inventor building detection tools and enhancing compass capabilities

#### Gasket (Agent G) - "Broken Prodigy"
- **Background:** Former compass master haunted by mysterious "incident"
- **Role:** Provides cryptic insights and fragmented guidance
- **Personality:** Intuitively understands compass anomalies, reluctant mentor with personal stake
- **Compass Role:** Gruff holographic mentor warning about anomaly and manipulation risks

## üß≠ Crimson Compass - Core Artifact

### Aesthetic Contrast
- **Visual Design:** Sleek quantum navigation device resembling pirate treasure
- **Tech Contrast:** Cutting-edge holographics with brass casing and glowing runes
- **Personality:** Evolving AI that chooses its users, haunted by Gasket's past

### Functional Mechanics
- **Primary Function:** Reveals "truth vectors" through deception fields
- **Evolution:** Device with personality that adapts to user and situation
- **Integration:** Central to all investigations, provides puzzle mechanics and narrative hints

## üìã Development Guidelines

### Episode Structure Standards
- **Format:** Consistent 3-scene structure with mini-puzzles and dialogue
- **Balance:** 95% case closure with 5% shadow unease
- **Integration:** Crimson Compass mechanics in every episode
- **Characters:** Standardized ensemble with episode-specific focus

### Quality Assurance
- **Consistency:** All episodes follow established character dynamics and mechanics
- **Polish:** Clean dialogue, engaging puzzles, cinematic moments
- **Testing:** Verify compass integration and dual narrative balance

### Content Expansion
- **Dialogue Enhancement:** Add character-specific banter and personality-driven interactions
- **Puzzle Depth:** Include multiple solution paths and environmental interactions
- **Visual Assets:** Ensure all scenes have corresponding production prompts
