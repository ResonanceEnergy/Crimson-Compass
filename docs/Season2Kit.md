# Crimson Compass — Series Foundation + Season 2 Kit

**Season 2 Theme:** Supply Chain Ghosts

## S02_SEAMLINE (working title)

### Theme Lens (Case Hunt)
Supply chain infiltration: manufacturing, logistics, vendor networks, component sourcing.

### Shadow Motifs (Season 2)
- **Motif A:** Component substitution (legitimate parts replaced with compromised ones)
- **Motif B:** Logistics diversion (shipments rerouted through shadow facilities)
- **Motif C:** Vendor credential harvesting (supplier access used for broader network penetration)

### Twists
- **Twist 1 (Ep 6–7):** Recontextualization — supply chain attacks are actually testing runs for larger operation
- **Twist 2 (Ep 12):** Hindsight Lock — all substitutions form a distributed computing network

### Gasket cadence
- Appears: Ep 3, Ep 7, Ep 11
- Fragments: Ep 7 and Ep 11

### Forbidden lever
- One optional catastrophic **G** choice recommended in Ep 11
