# Crimson Compass Season 2: Supply Chain Ghosts - Visual Asset Prompts

**Theme:** Logistics manipulation and vendor infiltration in a global spy thriller. Focus on industrial, corporate, and transportation aesthetics with subtle espionage elements.

---

## Backgrounds & Environments

### Urban Logistics Hubs
**Port Terminal Warehouse (Night)**
- Massive cargo containers stacked like Tetris blocks under sodium lights
- Forklifts and cranes moving silently in the background
- Security cameras mounted on rusted poles, fog rolling in from the water
- Chain-link fences with barbed wire, dimly lit office trailers
- Color palette: industrial blues and oranges, high contrast shadows

**Corporate Distribution Center (Day)**
- Clean, sterile warehouse with fluorescent lighting
- Pallet racks stretching to the ceiling, conveyor belts humming
- Loading docks with semi-trucks backed in, workers in safety vests
- Glass-walled offices overlooking the floor, motivational posters on walls
- Color palette: cool whites and grays with accent corporate blues

### International Transit Points
**Airport Cargo Facility**
- Baggage carousels repurposed for packages, customs scanners beeping
- Multilingual signage in various scripts, time zone clocks on walls
- Cargo planes visible through large windows, fuel trucks on tarmac
- Security checkpoints with X-ray machines, bored inspectors
- Color palette: institutional greens and yellows, sterile lighting

**Border Crossing Warehouse**
- Dusty frontier outpost, chain-link and concrete barriers
- Inspection booths with mirrors checking under vehicles
- Cargo manifests scattered on desks, coffee-stained paperwork
- Armed guards patrolling, suspicious drivers waiting in line
- Color palette: desert tans and military greens, harsh sunlight

### Hidden Operations Centers
**Abandoned Factory Floor**
- Rusted machinery frozen in time, cobwebs and dust motes
- Broken windows letting in shafts of light, graffiti on walls
- Makeshift command center with laptops and satellite phones
- Stacks of counterfeit shipping labels, forged manifests
- Color palette: decayed metallics and urban decay browns

## Objects & Props

### Gadgets & Tools
**MicroCam Drone**
- Tiny quadcopter with camera lens, LED indicators blinking
- Foldable wings, magnetic docking base
- Materials: carbon fiber and brushed aluminum
- Use: Aerial reconnaissance of warehouses and ports

**SignalTap Device**
- Handheld scanner with antenna array, digital display
- Rubberized grip, battery indicator glowing
- Materials: tactical polymer with metal accents
- Use: Intercepting wireless communications in logistics centers

**Passport Forge Kit**
- Compact briefcase with laminator, UV light, and document templates
- Hidden compartments for inks and holographic overlays
- Materials: Leather exterior, precision tools inside
- Use: Creating false identities for border crossings

### Clues & Evidence
**Altered Shipping Manifest**
- Crumpled document with redacted sections, coffee stains
- Barcode partially scratched, timestamps modified
- Details: Container numbers, destinations, cargo descriptions
- Visual: Fax-quality print with suspicious erasures

**Tire Track Cast**
- Plaster mold of vehicle tracks, ruler for scale
- Mud and gravel embedded, directional arrows drawn
- Details: Tread pattern analysis, weight calculations
- Visual: Forensic evidence kit, labeled and dated

**Compromised Invoice**
- Digital receipt with embedded malware watermark
- Vendor logo slightly off, payment routing suspicious
- Details: Itemized charges, bank account numbers
- Visual: Printed on thermal paper, partially torn

### Environmental Props
**Cargo Container Maze**

- Details: Door latches, ventilation slats, stacking locks

**Conveyor Belt System**

## Puzzles & Interactions

### Deduction Interface
**Hypothesis Board**
- Corkboard with pinned photos, string connecting clues
- Suspect mugshots, location maps, method diagrams
- Interactive: Drag-and-drop connections, elimination markers
- Visual: Detective noir style, digital overlay on physical board

**Timeline Widget**
- Horizontal scrollable timeline with event markers
- Time stamps, location pins, suspect movements
- Interactive: Zoom in/out, filter by category
- Visual: Digital forensics interface, glowing data points

### Mobile Interactions
**Swipe-to-Eliminate**
- Suspect cards fanned out, swipe left to disprove
- Animation: Card flies off screen with "X" mark
- Visual: Playing card style, holographic effects
- Feedback: Satisfying snap sound, particle effects

**Pinch-to-Zoom Maps**
- Interactive world map with supply chain routes
- Pinch gestures reveal hidden connections
- Visual: Satellite imagery with overlay graphics
- Details: Trade routes, shipping lanes, border crossings

### Puzzle Mechanics
**Container Code Breaker**
- Rotating dials with shipping container codes

- Interactive: Spin dials to match manifest data
- Visual: Mechanical safe with digital display

**Network Tracer**

- Highlighted paths, suspicious routing patterns
- Interactive: Tap nodes to reveal information
- Visual: Cybersecurity interface, data flow animations

## Art Style Guidelines

**Overall Aesthetic:** Clean vector graphics with subtle textures, mobile-optimized. Combine corporate realism with spy thriller stylization.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Navy blues, steel grays, warning oranges
- Accents: Electric blues for tech, blood reds for danger
- Neutrals: Industrial silvers, concrete grays, paper whites

**Technical Specs:**
- Mobile-first: 1080x1920 canvas, scalable vector assets
- Animation: Smooth 60fps, gesture-responsive interactions
- Accessibility: High contrast options, clear iconography

**Reference Inspirations:**
- Deus Ex (corporate dystopia)
- Papers, Please (bureaucratic interfaces)
- Hitman (environmental storytelling)
