# S01E01: Welcome Packet

## Episode Overview
**Title:** Welcome Packet
**Case:** Contractor access ring siphons early builds
**Case Token:** Ring leader arrested; prototype recovered
**Shadow Token:** Access logs skewed by recurring offset
**Theme:** Introduction to the agency, team dynamics, and deduction mechanics

## Adventure Game Structure

### Starting Location: Agency Briefing Room
**Description:** A cluttered office space with desks, filing cabinets, and computer terminals. Your character stands in the center, ready to begin the investigation.

**Interactive Objects:**
- **Desk Terminal** (OBSERVE): Agency database access point
- **Filing Cabinet** (OBSERVE): Contains case files and personnel records
- **Coffee Machine** (ENGAGE): Provides temporary morale boost
- **Door to Hallway** (MOVE): Exit to main corridor

**Initial Inventory:**
- Agency ID Badge
- Digital Tablet
- Encrypted Communicator

### Key Characters (Text-Based Dialogue)

#### Helix (Administrative Coordinator)
**Description:** A young woman in professional attire, managing multiple tablets and headsets.
**Dialogue Tree:**
- Greeting: "Welcome to Crimson Compass, Closer. I'm Helix - I coordinate operations and keep everyone from going rogue."
- About the case: "We've got a contractor ring stealing prototype tech. Your first assignment."
- Team info: "You'll work with Optimus (intel), ZTech (tech), and sometimes Gasket (unofficial consultant)."

#### Optimus (Intelligence Analyst)
**Description:** Middle-aged man with data glasses, surrounded by computer screens.
**Dialogue Tree:**
- Analysis: "Access logs show 47 unauthorized entries. Pattern suggests insider involvement."
- Evidence: "Vehicle registrations cross-reference with contractor database. One match found."
- Warning: "Probability of armed resistance: 23%. Exercise caution."

#### ZTech (Technical Specialist)
**Description:** Disheveled genius with multiple gadgets and computer equipment.
**Dialogue Tree:**
- Tech scan: "Thermal imaging shows building overheating. Prototype cooling system compromised."
- Hacking: "Their security is outdated. I can bypass it, but it'll leave traces."
- Gadgets: "I've got scanners, lockpicks, and surveillance drones ready."

#### Gasket (Unofficial Consultant)
**Description:** Weathered older man in casual attire, appears intermittently.
**Dialogue Tree:**
- Intuition: "Something's wrong here. The setup feels like a trap."
- Cryptic advice: "Trust your instincts. Not everything is as it seems."
- Experience: "I've seen operations like this before. They always go sideways."

## Scene 1: Stakeout Introduction

### Location: Urban Rooftop
**Description:** Overlooking corporate headquarters at dusk. City lights below, construction crane in distance.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Telescope** (OBSERVE): View street activity below
- **Radio Equipment** (ENGAGE): Contact team members
- **Ventilation Duct** (MOVE): Access to building interior
- **Fire Escape** (MOVE): Alternative descent route

**Puzzle: Surveillance Setup**
- **Objective:** Position observation equipment for optimal coverage
- **Mechanics:** Arrange telescope, camera, and listening device
- **Solution:** Place telescope at edge, camera on tripod, listening device near air vent
- **Reward:** Clear view of target emergence

**Dialogue Encounters:**
- **Helix (Radio):** "Closer, target emerging from building. Maintain position and observe."
- **Optimus (Radio):** "Movement pattern detected. Evasion training likely. Stay hidden."
- **ZTech (Radio):** "Thermal scan active. Building's running hot - prototype issues?"

## Scene 2: Warehouse District Investigation

### Location: Abandoned Warehouse District
**Description:** Dim streetlights, chain-link fences, graffiti-covered buildings.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Suspicious Vehicles** (OBSERVE): Three parked cars to examine
- **Chain-link Fence** (KIT): Use wire cutters to create opening
- **Warehouse Door** (PROTOCOL): Lockpicking or security bypass
- **Cargo Container** (ENGAGE): May contain evidence or hiding spot

**Puzzle: Vehicle Identification**
- **Objective:** Match license plates to contractor database
- **Mechanics:** Use tablet to scan and cross-reference plates
- **Solution:** One vehicle belongs to target company (partial plate: "XC-47")
- **Clue:** Database shows full registration for matching vehicle

**Combat Encounter (Optional):**
- **Hostile Guards** (ENGAGE): Two security personnel
- **Strategy:** Use environment (crates, shadows) for advantage
- **Resolution:** Subdue without lethal force or avoid confrontation

## Scene 3: Confrontation and Resolution

### Location: Warehouse Interior
**Description:** Dimly lit storage area with crates, computer terminals, and prototype equipment.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Computer Terminal** (ENGAGE): Access security logs
- **Prototype Device** (OBSERVE): Examine stolen technology
- **Security Camera** (KIT): Disable surveillance
- **Evidence Locker** (PROTOCOL): Bypass electronic lock

**Puzzle: Access Log Analysis**
- **Type:** Pattern Recognition
- **Description:** Examine logs showing 11-minute time offsets
- **Solution:** Identify deliberate manipulation pattern
- **Reward:** Shadow Token insight

**Final Confrontation:**
- **Ring Leader:** Corporate executive with smug attitude
- **Dialogue Options:**
  - Intimidate: "Your operation ends here."
  - Question: "Who ordered the theft?"
  - Evidence: "We have your access logs."

### Key Items to Collect:
- **Wire Cutters** (From agency supply room)
- **Lockpick Set** (Provided by ZTech)
- **Thermal Scanner** (ZTech gadget)
- **Encrypted Drive** (Evidence from warehouse)

### Item Combinations:
- **Wire Cutters + Chain-link Fence** = Access to restricted area
- **Lockpick Set + Warehouse Door** = Silent entry
- **Thermal Scanner + Prototype Device** = Diagnostic information

## Gameplay Flow

1. **Introduction:** Meet team in briefing room, receive assignment
2. **Stakeout:** Position equipment, observe target emergence
3. **Pursuit:** Follow to warehouse district, identify vehicles
4. **Investigation:** Enter warehouse, gather evidence
5. **Confrontation:** Face ring leader, resolve situation
6. **Debrief:** Return to agency, analyze findings

## Difficulty Options

- **Easy:** More obvious clues, additional hints from team
- **Normal:** Standard puzzle difficulty, balanced combat
- **Hard:** Minimal clues, challenging encounters, time pressure

## Achievement Opportunities

- **Silent Infiltration:** Complete warehouse entry without detection
- **Evidence Master:** Find all hidden clues and documents
- **Team Coordinator:** Use all team member skills effectively
- **Quick Resolution:** Complete episode in under 30 minutes
