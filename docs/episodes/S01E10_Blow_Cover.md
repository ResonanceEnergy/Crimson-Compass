# S01E10: Blow Cover

## Episode Overview
**Title:** Blow Cover
**Case:** Theft ring embedded in corporate security
**Case Token:** Ring disrupted (any path)
**Shadow Token:** Skew clusters around custody initiation
**Theme:** Deep infiltration, catastrophic choices, pattern revelation

## Scene 1: Ring Network Mapping

### Location: Corporate Security Headquarters
**Description:** Security operations center with monitoring stations, employee areas, and administrative offices.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Security Database** (ENGAGE): Access personnel records
- **Communication Logs** (OBSERVE): Analyze message patterns
- **Surveillance Feeds** (ENGAGE): Monitor suspicious activity
- **Employee Lounge** (ENGAGE): Gather informal intelligence

**Puzzle: Network Mapping**
- **Objective:** Identify all ring members and their connections within security
- **Mechanics:** Cross-reference communication patterns with personnel data
- **Solution:** Hidden communication channels reveal full organizational structure
- **Clue:** Unusual meeting patterns in security logs indicate secret coordination

**Infiltration Tactics:**
- **Social Engineering:** Gather information through conversations
- **Digital Surveillance:** Monitor communications and access logs
- **Physical Observation:** Track suspicious personnel movements

## Scene 2: Catastrophic Decision Point

### Location: Emergency Operations Center
**Description:** Crisis management facility with security feeds, communication systems, and tactical displays.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Command Console** (ENGAGE): Coordinate disruption operation
- **Security Overrides** (PROTOCOL): Control facility systems
- **Communication Arrays** (ENGAGE): Contact allied forces
- **Emergency Protocols** (KIT): Access contingency plans

**Puzzle: Ring Disruption Strategy**
- **Objective:** Choose the optimal method to dismantle the embedded theft ring
- **Mechanics:** Evaluate multiple disruption paths with different consequences
- **Solution:** Path-dependent - surgical strike, mass arrest, or total reset
- **Clue:** Custody timing patterns reveal operational clusters

**Moral Choices:**
- **Surgical Strike:** Precise takedown minimizing collateral damage
- **Mass Arrest:** Comprehensive sweep with higher risk of leaks
- **Burn Approach:** Total operation reset with maximum disruption

## Scene 3: Consequences and Revelation

### Location: Multiple Outcome Locations
**Description:** Varies based on chosen disruption path - either secure facility, extraction point, or reset scenario.

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Evidence Lockers** (ENGAGE): Secure recovered intelligence
- **Communication Terminals** (ENGAGE): Report operation results
- **Containment Systems** (PROTOCOL): Control facility lockdown
- **Extraction Vehicles** (MOVE): Emergency escape routes

**Puzzle: Pattern Revelation**
- **Objective:** Connect the ring's activities to larger organizational patterns
- **Mechanics:** Analyze skew clusters around custody initiation events
- **Solution:** Reveals systematic manipulation of evidence handling
- **Clue:** Timing anomalies cluster around critical decision points

**Resolution Outcomes:**
- **Controlled Takedown:** Ring disrupted with minimal exposure
- **Mass Arrest:** Comprehensive capture but increased operational risk
- **Catastrophic Reset:** Complete operation burn with fresh start opportunity
