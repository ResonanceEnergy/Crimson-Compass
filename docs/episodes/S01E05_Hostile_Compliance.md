## Episode Overview
**Title:** Hostile Compliance  
**Case:** Corporate counsel legally blocks investigative access  
**Case Token:** Access restored; obstruction routed around  
**Shadow Token:** Legacy clause forces evidence into sealed track  
**Theme:** Legal warfare, bureaucratic obstruction

## Scene 1: Legal Battleground

**Background:** Corporate boardroom, mahogany table, legal documents scattered. City skyline through floor-to-ceiling windows.

**Narrative:** You've arrived at corporate headquarters where legal counsel invokes privacy laws to prevent access to the investigation site.

**Puzzle:** Legal Precedent Matching  
- **Objective:** Find applicable legal loopholes  
- **Mechanics:** Document database search, case matching  
- **Solution:** Emergency provision allows access under national security clause  
- **Clue:** Recently updated regulation with oversight gap

**Dialogue:**

**Counsel:** "I'm afraid I must insist. Our privacy policies are quite clear."

**Helix:** "And our warrant is clearer. Let's not make this difficult."

**Optimus:** "Legal database shows 47 similar cases. Success rate: 89%."

## Scene 2: Evidence Access

**Background:** Restricted server room, legal observers present, security personnel monitoring. Tense atmosphere.

**Narrative:** Inside the restricted area, you find evidence of tampering, but counsel demands you leave immediately.

**Puzzle:** Evidence Preservation  
- **Objective:** Document evidence before legal removal  
- **Mechanics:** Rapid scanning, priority assessment  
- **Solution:** Critical files backed up to secure server  
- **Clue:** Hidden metadata timestamps

**Dialogue:**

**Legal Assistant:** "You have exactly 15 minutes before we invoke the restraining clause."

**ZTech:** "Fifteen minutes? I can clone their entire server in ten!"

**Security Chief:** "This is highly irregular. I don't like being caught in the middle."
