# S01E07: The Convenient Suspect

## Episode Overview
**Title:** The Convenient Suspect  
**Case:** Decoy culprit frame  
**Case Token:** Sabotage prevented; decoy arrested  
**Shadow Token:** Exit plan staged before the crime  
**Theme:** False flags, misdirection, pattern recognition  

## Character Descriptions

### Player (The Closer)
- **Appearance:** Skeptical expression, examining evidence carefully
- **Background:** Learning to question obvious solutions
- **Personality:** Analytical, suspicious of convenience

- **Helix:** Interviewing witnesses and suspects

## Scene 1: Suspect Interrogation

**Background:** Interrogation room, two-way mirror, recording equipment. Stark lighting, uncomfortable chairs.

**Narrative:** The suspect in custody appears to be the perfect culprit - all evidence points to them, but something feels staged.

**Puzzle:** Alibi Verification  
- **Objective:** Cross-reference suspect's timeline with evidence  
- **Mechanics:** Timeline reconstruction, witness correlation  
- **Solution:** Suspect's alibi has unexplained gaps  
- **Clue:** Digital footprints show fabricated activity

**Dialogue:**

**Decoy Suspect:** "I don't understand. I was just doing my job."

**Helix:** "And your job included accessing restricted systems? Convenient."

**Optimus:** "Evidence chain is too perfect. Statistical anomaly detected."

## Scene 2: Frame Job Revelation

**Background:** Evidence locker, organized shelves, examination tables. Forensic equipment, analysis screens.

**Narrative:** You've uncovered the framing operation. The decoy was planted to cover the real sabotage attempt.

**Puzzle:** Evidence Chain Deconstruction  
- **Objective:** Identify planted vs genuine evidence  
- **Mechanics:** Chain of custody verification, authenticity testing  
- **Solution:** Multiple pieces trace to same source  
- **Clue:** Chemical residue on planted evidence

**Dialogue:**

**Forensic Tech:** "These fingerprints? Too clean. Like they were pressed on purpose."

**ZTech:** "Digital trail leads to a ghost account. Professional job."

**Real Culprit:** "You think you understand, but you're just seeing the surface."

## Puzzles & Mechanics

### Timeline Reconstruction
- **Type:** Evidence Correlation

- **Reward:** Frame identification
