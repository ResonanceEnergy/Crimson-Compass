## Scene 1: Calibration Briefing

**Background:** Agency briefing room with holographic displays and ergonomic furniture that adjusts to user mood. The Crimson Compass sits on the central table, its brass casing gleaming under the lights, pulsing with quantum energy.

**Narrative:** Optimus has called a mandatory "compass calibration session" after your first month of field work. The device has been showing inconsistent readings, and Helix has prepared a comprehensive training manual.

**Crimson Compass Mini-Puzzle:** Basic Calibration Gestures
- **Objective:** Follow holographic Gasket's hands to perform fundamental calibration gestures
- **Mechanics:** Match gesture sequence perfectly, compass provides humorous failure animations and tutorial hints
- **Solution:** Successful calibration unlocks "Echo Detection" mode for temporal anomaly scanning
- **Clue:** Compass responds to each team member's unique interaction style

**Dialogue:**

**Optimus:** "Player. The compass has been... temperamental. Showing readings that don't match our databases. Probability of user error: 67%. Or is it the device malfunctioning again?"

**Helix:** "Now, now, Optimus. The compass just needs proper calibration. I've prepared a 47-page manual with coffee breaks scheduled. And Player - how's it been treating you? Any more of those sea shanty interludes?"

**ZTech:** "Ooh, calibration! I built this calibrator drone - it sings show tunes while adjusting quantum matrices! Maximum absurdity, maximum precision! Watch this!"

**Gasket:** [Holographic form flickering to life] "Calibration... it's not just about the device. It's about the user. The compass chooses who it trusts. Remember that, kid."

**Player:** "The compass keeps showing me these tutorial flashbacks - visions of your hands performing these same gestures, Gasket. Is it teaching me... or testing me?"

**Compass Interface:** [Activates with holographic tutorial] The compass projects Gasket's hands performing calibration gestures from years ago, accompanied by a jaunty sea shanty whistle.

## Scene 2: Field Calibration Operation

**Background:** Quantum research facility exterior - sleek, modern architecture with security drones patrolling. Rain-slicked surfaces reflect neon lights, compass navigation vectors overlay the scene.

**Narrative:** A tip leads you to a research facility where industrial spies are attempting to steal quantum research. The compass readings are erratic, requiring on-site calibration to identify the stolen prototype.

**Crimson Compass Mini-Puzzle:** Quantum Signature Scanning
- **Objective:** Use calibrated compass to scan prototype locations and identify temporal anomalies
- **Mechanics:** Scan 4 prototype locations, cross-reference with facility database through truth vectors
- **Solution:** One signature shows impossible "echo" matching Gasket's past incident site
- **Clue:** Compass overlay reveals temporal distortions around the target prototype

**Puzzle:** Quantum Signature Matching
- **Objective:** Match energy signatures to identify the stolen prototype using compass verification
- **Mechanics:** Use compass to scan 4 prototype locations, cross-reference with facility database
- **Solution:** One signature shows impossible "echo" - matching a location from Gasket's incident
- **Clue:** Compass overlay shows temporal distortions around the target

**Dialogue:**

**Helix:** "Okay team, let's calibrate and coordinate. Optimus - try not to yell at the compass this time. ZTech - no show tunes until we're inside. Player, you're on point with the compass."

**Optimus:** "These readings... they suggest temporal anomalies. Or perhaps I need new data glasses. Probability of equipment malfunction: 89%. Compass, respond! This is not a rotary phone!"

**ZTech:** "Temporal anomalies? That's my jam! I built a temporal flux detector last week. It also makes toast! And sings! Maximum absurdity, minimum calories!"

**Player:** "Compass is picking up something weird. One of these signatures has an 'echo' - like it's existing in two places at once. And the location... it matches one of Gasket's old incident sites."

**Gasket:** [Holographic whisper directly to Player] "The echoes... they're not random. The compass is showing you my path. But why now? What game is being played?"

**Helix:** "Focus, team! The compass calibration is working - we're getting cleaner readings. Player, guide us to the target. ZTech, keep your flux detector quiet - we don't want to toast the evidence."

## Scene 3: Resolution and Echo Discovery

**Background:** Facility interior - sterile white corridors with quantum containment fields humming. Compass projections map the spy network while revealing impossible echo locations.

**Narrative:** The calibrated compass reveals the spy network's full extent, leading to a clean bust. But the final reading shows an impossible "echo location" that matches one of Gasket's incident sites.

**Crimson Compass Mini-Puzzle:** Echo Location Verification
- **Objective:** Verify the impossible echo location through compass truth vector confirmation
- **Mechanics:** Compass confirms temporal anomaly matches Gasket's past incident exactly
- **Solution:** Echo location verified as real, hinting at deeper connections to Gasket's history
- **Clue:** Compass shows truth vector pointing to coordinates that "shouldn't exist"

**Dialogue:**

**Optimus:** "Network analysis complete. Spy ring dismantled. Probability of complete recovery: 94%. But this echo location... it defies temporal logic."

**Helix:** "Good work, team! The calibration paid off. Player, your compass work was spot-on. ZTech, your flux detector actually helped for once."

**ZTech:** "For once? Please! My inventions are always brilliant! Though the toast function did burn the evidence locker... just a little."

**Player:** "The compass is still showing that echo. It's not a malfunction - it's pointing to a location that matches Gasket's incident site exactly. What does this mean?"

**Gasket:** [Holographic form intensifying] "It means the past isn't as buried as I thought. The compass is connecting dots I tried to erase. Be careful what you dig up, kid."

**Helix:** "We'll investigate the echo location next episode. For now, case closed. But... that coordinate hint the compass just displayed? That's concerning."
