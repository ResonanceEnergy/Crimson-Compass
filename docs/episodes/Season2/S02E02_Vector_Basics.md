**Gasket:** [Holographic warning] "Fractures aren't accidents. They're weapons. The data echoes... they're hiding something. Don't trust what you see in the fractures."

**Helix:** "Good vector work, team. The breach is contained, but these fracture points... they shouldn't exist. Compass, what are you showing us?"

## Scene 3: Vector Analysis and Fracture Concerns

**Background:** Agency analysis room with vector playback holograms, fracture point recordings displaying spatial distortions.

**Narrative:** The team analyzes the fracture anomalies discovered during the investigation. The compass reveals these aren't random glitches but deliberate reality manipulations.

**Crimson Compass Mini-Puzzle:** Fracture Pattern Analysis
- **Objective:** Analyze fracture point patterns to understand their deliberate nature
- **Mechanics:** Compass overlays truth vectors on fracture recordings, reveals manipulation signatures
- **Solution:** Fractures show identical patterns to Gasket's past incident signatures
- **Clue:** Compass confirms fractures are artificial, not natural anomalies

**Dialogue:**

**Optimus:** "Fracture analysis complete. Patterns show deliberate manipulation, not random quantum events. Probability of artificial creation: 91%."

**Helix:** "These fractures match patterns from Gasket's incident files. Someone is using the same techniques. Player, your vector navigation was perfect."

**ZTech:** "Artificial fractures? I can build a fracture neutralizer! It'll also juggle! Maximum problem-solving, minimum collateral damage!"

**Player:** "The compass vectors cut right through the fractures like they knew they were coming. Gasket, these patterns... they're from your past?"

**Gasket:** [Intensifying hologram] "My past... or someone using my techniques. These fractures aren't just breaches. They're doors. And something's coming through."

**Helix:** "We'll investigate further. For now, the data is recovered. But these fracture points... they change everything we know about reality."
