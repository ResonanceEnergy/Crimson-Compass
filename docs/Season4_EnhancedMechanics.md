# Crimson Compass Season 4 Enhanced Mechanics

## Overview
Season 4 "Decoys Engineered to be Solvable" introduces advanced mechanics centered around deception detection, false trail analysis, and jurisdictional complexity. Building upon the core mechanics established in previous seasons, Season 4 adds sophisticated tools for navigating engineered misdirection and shadow operations.

### Recovery Pattern Scanner
**Purpose:** Analyzes recovery operations that may mask true decoy purposes, detecting when "successful" operations are actually elaborate distractions.

**Capabilities:**

## Timing Analyzers & Jurisdictional Puzzles

### Decoy Timing Analyzer
**Purpose:** Analyzes precise timing patterns in decoy operations, false flags, and synchronized distractions.

**Capabilities:**
- **Temporal Patterning:** Maps synchronization between multiple false operations
- **Delay Analysis:** Identifies deliberate timing gaps designed to mislead
- **Synchronization Detection:** Finds coordinated deception campaigns

**Risks & Challenges:**
- **Temporal Paranoia:** Over-analysis can lead to false conclusions
- **Over-synchronization:** May detect patterns where none exist

### Jurisdictional Authority Mapper
**Purpose:** Maps complex jurisdictional overlaps and authority conflicts created by international decoy operations.

**Capabilities:**
- **Authority Chain Analysis:** Traces command hierarchies across jurisdictions
- **Jurisdiction Validation:** Verifies legal authority in multi-national scenarios
- **Conflict Resolution:** Provides pathways through jurisdictional mazes

**Complexity Levels:**
- **Simple:** Clear authority hierarchies
- **Complex:** Overlapping jurisdictions with clear resolution paths
- **Byzantine:** Deliberately confusing authority structures requiring puzzle-solving

### Authority Resolution Puzzle
**Purpose:** Interactive puzzle mechanic for resolving jurisdictional conflicts in decoy operations.

**Puzzle Types:**
- **Authority Chain:** Reconstruct proper command hierarchies
- **Jurisdiction Overlap:** Resolve conflicting claims to authority
- **Conflict Resolution:** Find legal pathways through jurisdictional deadlocks

**Mechanics:**
- Time-limited puzzle resolution
- Hint system for complex scenarios
- Progressive difficulty scaling
