# Crimson Compass Saga: The Screenplay

**FADE IN:**

**EXT. FORGOTTEN CITY UNDERBELLY - NIGHT**

Neon flickers. AGENT [PLAYER NAME] (30s, rugged) wakes in an alley, no memory. A cracked device GLOWS.

**GASKET (V.O.)**  
Humanity detected. Welcome to the Compass.

Helix (40s, sharp) appears. OPTIMUS (30s, deadpan) and ZTECH (50s, eccentric) flank her.

**HELIX**  
You're the closer. We prevent collapses. Gadgets, paperwork, luck.

**OPTIMUS**  
Luck's on our side. Mostly.

ZTECH hands a suitcase. Gadgets: drone orders pizza, lockpick recites Shakespeare.

**CUT TO:**

**INT. LONDON TUBE - DAY**

Chase! Contractors siphon prototypes. Agent dodges security.

**OPTIMUS**  
Plan A: Shoot. Plan B: Shoot better.

Gadget fails: Lockpick quotes poetry instead of opening.

**GASKET (V.O.)**  
Patterns... not right.

**CUT TO:**

**EXT. PARIS ROOFTOPS - NIGHT**

Biotech sting. Helix hints at affair. Optimus's debts force Macau escape.

**HELIX**  
Efficiency is laziness with PR.

ZTech's tool backfires: Suction gloves stick too well.

**CUT TO:**

**INT. TOKYO NEON JUNGLE - NIGHT**

Heist. Bad guy: Charming arms dealer.

Chase through alleys. Gadget: Cloaking device glows.

**CUT TO:**

**EXT. DUBAI DESERT - DAWN**

Infiltration. Burj Khalifa climb. Magnetic boots.

**OPTIMUS**  
Luck paradox.

Twist: Case resolves too early. Gasket glitches.

**GASKET**  
You're benchmarking.

Vision: Same op elsewhere.

**CUT TO:**

**INT. COMPASS HQ - NIGHT**

Finale. Anomaly dismantled. Pattern reappears.

**GASKET**  
Your choices fed the machine.

Betrayal: Advisors' lies. Architect implied.

**HELIX**  
We won... but nothing changed.

**FADE OUT.**

**THE END (Season 1)**
